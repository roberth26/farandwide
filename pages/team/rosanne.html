Having the pleasure to have worked in the Entertainment Industry for over 20 years, Rosanne Howell started in Post Production Audio where she worked on television shows like, “The Big Comfy Couch” and feature films like, “Jakob Two Two and the Hooded Fang”. Moving into Production roles, Rosanne enjoyed a team win at the Emmy’s for the pre-school animated series, “Mike the Knight”. Alongside and related to Film and Television is Music Production.

Rosanne has led and organized many events, including concerts for charity. She has also volunteered at the Ontario Provincial Advocate for Children and Youth, specifically for Youth Leaving Care, and she had the privilege to have volunteered at the Tupac Amaru Shakur Center for the Arts in Stone Mountain, Georgia, where she taught music, music technology and assisted in their annual theatrical production. Rosanne is now working in video games with successes like, “Watch Dogs: Legion”, “Assassin‘s Creed Valhalla” and “Far Cry 5”.

Rosanne is also leading a remote team in the production of the animated short film, “Inspire”, and is in search of her next project.
